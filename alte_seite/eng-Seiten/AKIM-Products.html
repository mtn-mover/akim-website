Product Overview.

We build standard gears and develop special, custom-made gears.

We utilize four construction principles:
•  All of our gearboxes are symmetrical, compact and coaxial.
•  All of our gearboxes allow for a high ratio into the smallest space.
•  All of our gearboxes have lifetime lubrication and are therefore maintenance free.
•  All of our standard gearboxes are built with a modular system.

ACBAR Eccentric Gears.

ACBAR eccentric gears are indestructible and allow greatest gear ratios in minimal space.

What ACBAR eccentric gears especially characterized.

•  Compact and indestructible
•  Ratios up to 13‘600:1 single stage
•  Almost any ratio feasible
•  Flange or foot case housing (mounting)
•  Direct motor mounting
•  Smooth operation
•  Custom design available

We build 6 sizes.

•  Type 11 – 80 Nm
•  Type 81 – 150 Nm
•  Type 121 – 250 Nm
•  Type 12/451 – 450 Nm
•  Type 251 – 600 Nm
•  Type 501 – 1'000 Nm

Two Discs Gears.

AKIM two-Disc Gears are robust, strong and reliable – even for rough use. They are available in two versions: with output side shaft end or with hollow output shaft to put on.

What AKIM two-disc gears especially characterized.

•  Robust and reliable
•  Fixed-ratios up to 125:1, single-stage
•  Multi stage gearboxes on request
•  Low or adjustable backlash
•  Nominal torque up to 20‘000 Nm
•  High overload-safety
•  Smooth operation
•  Easy motor mounting (Separate flange with coupling)
•  Custom design available

What really sets AKIM two-disc-slip-on and plug-on-gears apart from others is.

•  Compact construction
•  Easy and direct integration into customer’s machines
•  Adjustable backlash < 0.02°
•  Efficiency up to 90%
•  Nominal torque up to 5'000 Nm
•  Tapered-roller-bearing-supported output
•  High overload-safety
•  Low weight
•  Smooth operation

We build 8 sizes.
